Saudi Aramco is one of the world’s largest integrated energy and chemicals companies.

Its Upstream operations manages Saudi Arabia's unique hydrocarbon reserve base of 251.2 billion barrels of oil equivalent (as of the end of 2023), optimizing production and maximizing long-term value.

It also operates a strategically-integrated global Downstream business. Headquartered in the city of Dhahran, the company operates within the Kingdom and worldwide, and employs more than 73,000 people.
